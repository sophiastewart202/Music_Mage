From those retro record hits...

Music Mage is a music recommendation system that finds music for you based on a single sample track that you provide. Using Spotify's API, we retrieve data on your song, put it into a vector, and compare it with thousands of other songs in our database. The closer the vector values are, the more similar the songs are in style, sound, and mood.

To the top pop singles...

After sorting all these songs according to how closely they match your sample track, we then sort them by popularity to make sure we are giving you the best possible results. Of these, we return to you the top five matches, hoping that at least one or two of them you have never seen before.

Let the music magic begin!

Start by typing in the name of one your favorite song tracks into the Music Mage search box. Next, enter in the name of the artist, band, or ensemble that performed it. Sometimes popular classics have been recorded many times by multiple different artists, so don't skip this important piece! Finally, hit the 'Get Recommendations' button and check out the new song suggestions that Music Mage has for you!
